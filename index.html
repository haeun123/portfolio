Hi, I'm Holly - a recent graduate of Carnegie Mellon University. I recieved a Bachelors of Science in Psychology and minor in Human-Computer Interaction. I aim to leverage my background in cognitive science, programming, art, and psychological research. As an experience designer, I am driven by getting to the bottom of complex problems and creating robust solutions.

Feeding Frenzy

Educational Game design

We designed an educational, EDGE-framework guided board game to effectively teach middle to high school students about the balance of trophic interactions in the food web as well as the impact of human activities on ecology.

SHFIT App

Interaction design, Experience design, UI design

We tackled issues surrounding shift swapping, communication, accountability, and unstable scheduling in our design of a mobile app. Shfit is a digital platform where gym employees can swap shifts, message each other, and schedule their week efficiently and enjoyably.

Gibbor: AHAVA Memory Care Residence

Communication design, Information design

I worked with a team through contextual research, interviews and observations to identify problems and create Gibbor - a solution that would increase appreciation and empowerment of staff at the AHAVA Memory Care Residence and the satisfaction of its residents.

Treasure Run

Service design, Experience design

A "treasure hunt"-inspired service designed to build a stronger community by connecting new and old residents of Regent Square to each other and to the neighborhood.
