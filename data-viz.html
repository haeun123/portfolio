Air Quality and Asthma in Cities: Data Visualization (PAGE INCOMPLETE)

How do the levels of pollen and air quality index vary and compare over 5 days, for the top 5 and bottom 5 cities for asthma rates/hospitalizations?

Introduction

I designed and created a kinetic data visualization for asthma sufferers effectively conveying insightful patterns and trends in air quality and asthma trigger levels.

Timeline:

Spring 2019 (a few weeks)

Task

I (Course: Information Design) was tasked to create a data visualization exploring a question I have that utilizes visual design principles and effective data visualization.

My Role

I worked individually on all parts of the process, starting with data exploration, data collection, data analyzing, visualization, and subsequent interations.
