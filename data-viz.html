Air Quality and Asthma in Cities: Data Visualization

How do the levels of pollen and air quality index vary and compare over 5 days, for the top 5 and bottom 5 cities for asthma rates/hospitalizations?

Timeline:

Spring 2019 (a few weeks)

Course:

Information Design

Medium.com Documentation

Please visit my Medium post (and scroll all the way down) to read a more in depth process documentation.

Introduction

Using effective visual design principles and data visualization practices, I posed a high-level question- (What is the nature of the relationship between asthma prevalence and air quality in US cities?), defined and gathered the data to answer it, interpreted the data, and created a kinetic visualization to convey the "story" ir "message" behind the numbers. (Since this was for a time-constrained design project and not a research project, the meaning of the data had to be slightly less rigorously analyzed/interpreted in order to tell a compelling story.)

My Role

I worked individually on all parts of the process, starting with data exploration, data collection, data analyzing, visualization, and subsequent interations.
